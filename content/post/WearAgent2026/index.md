## Overview

We are organizing [WearAgent 2026](https://wearagent.github.io/), the 1st International Workshop on Interactive AI for Personal Wearable Agents. The workshop will be co-located with [UbiComp/ISWC 2026](https://www.ubicomp.org/) in Shanghai, China, on October 11-12, 2026.

WearAgent 2026 explores wearable agents that connect continuous sensing, foundation model reasoning, and proactive human-agent interaction. The workshop focuses on the shift from passive activity recognition toward proactive, agentic assistance grounded in wearable sensor streams and multimodal AI.

## Call for Papers

We invite contributions on topics including sensor-in-the-loop agents, proactive interaction design, on-device reasoning, activity and context understanding, human-agent collaboration, privacy and ethics, and systems or prototypes for smart glasses, hearables, smart clothing, and other wearable interfaces.

Submission types include full papers and demo/prototype submissions. All submissions will receive two single-blind reviews.

## Important Dates

- Submission deadline: June 19, 2026, 23:59 AOE, tentative
- Notification of acceptance: July 10, 2026, tentative
- Workshop date: October 11/12, 2026

For submission details and the latest updates, please visit the [WearAgent 2026 workshop website](https://wearagent.github.io/).
