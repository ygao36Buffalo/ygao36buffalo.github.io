## Overview

We are excited to share that two of our papers have been accepted to **Proceedings of the ACM on Interactive, Mobile, Wearable and Ubiquitous Technologies (ACM IMWUT)** and will be presented at **UbiComp 2026**.

- **HoloHand: Bidirectional Motion–Language Modeling for Semantic Hand Interaction in Immersive Environments**
- **EmbodiedRecall: A Ring-to-Glasses System for Preserving Valuable, Fleeting Moments in Daily Activities**

More details will be added as the final publication information and conference program become available.
