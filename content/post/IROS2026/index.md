## Overview

We are pleased to share that our paper, **Design of a Multimodal Companion Robot for Elderly Care: Enhancing Affective Intelligence with Context-Aware Enactments**, has been accepted to the **2026 IEEE/RSJ International Conference on Intelligent Robots and Systems (IROS 2026)**.

This work explores how multimodal companion robots can support elderly care through affective intelligence and context-aware enactments, contributing to our broader research agenda on human-robot interaction, affect-aware systems, and human-centered AI.

More details will be added as the conference program and final publication information become available.
